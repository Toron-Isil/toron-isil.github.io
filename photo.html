About Photographie

I like to take pictures

My dad gave me my first camera (still with rollfilm), from that moment on I took the camera with me on all joint ventures and took pictures diligently.

In the Year 2010 I began to use a SLR Digial Camera. The time of the rollfilm was over. Almost endless photosessions, from 36 pictures to> 1.000 and the possibility to see on the display whether the photo was successfully taken.

Combining digital drawing and editing photos.

Of course, I have previously edited photos on my computer, but they were only downloaded from the Internet or transmitted by scanner. Now with the digital camera, the world was open for editing - calibrating, retouching, rescaling, combining ... etc. of all the pictures.
